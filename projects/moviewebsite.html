Movie website Project

This project was one of my first ever Websites, about a fictional romcom movie with a splash of horror. My parter I collaborated with for the duration of this project and I shared a similar interest in romance movies, so we based our project off of one. It was so fun to work on and i found that choosing colors that complemented one another was quite therapeutic. This project was a lovely one to begin with, as it combined my learning experience with something I enjoyed, making it all the more fun.

The movie this project advertises stars two actors, Sam Claflin, and Emilia Clark, whom also starred as main characters in the hit movie "Me before You". This movie was one of my main inspirations for this project, along with another movie starring Jennifer Lopez that my partner discussed with me whilst brainstorming. In our "movie", Sam Calfin is a suspected killer and Emilia Clarke is the girl that just moved in to the neighborhood. As Sam and Emilia go through their escapades and misunderstandings, they begin to wonder if the chemistry between them is blooming love or a messy murder case. The contrasting themes of romance, comedy and horror made this such an interesting prompt to write on, and I could go on and on about how it can align into the perfect goldie.
